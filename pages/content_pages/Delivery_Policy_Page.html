“Delivery Policy”

New South Wales, Victoria, Queensland

Free Next-day Delivery is available on orders made before 2 PM and all orders over AUD$80 within the Metro Area. For orders below $80, the delivery fee is $15.

For deliveries to remote suburban areas, pricing varies by postal code. You can enter your postal code at cart page to view the applicable rate.

Australian Capital Territory

Due to rising operational costs, ACT orders over $150 will receive complimentary delivery. For orders below 150, a $30 fee applies.

Delivery Schedule

Deliveries of the most post codes are available from Monday to Friday with no deliveries on Weekends or Public Holidays.

When making an order, you can select your desired delivery date up to 21 days in advance.

Please note that daily delivery slots are limited. To increase the likelihood of securing your desired delivery date, we recommend placing your order as soon as you can.

We will send a text message with an Estimated Time of Arrival on your selected delivery date.

We apologize that we can't guarantee Exact Delivery Times. All orders are delivered between 9 am to 7 pm.

On rare occasions, our delivery window extends beyond 7 PM due to the traffic situation, and the high volume of orders on that day.

If you haven't received your ETA text message, you will receive it when your order is on its way.
